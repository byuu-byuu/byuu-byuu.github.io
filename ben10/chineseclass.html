Chinese Language Course

Here’s a detailed course outline for the Chinese Language Class on J Power Online Language Class:

Chinese Language Course Details
Course Name: General Chinese Course
Level: Beginner to Advanced
Duration: 3–6 months per level
Mode: Online (Live & Pre-recorded Classes)
Instructor: Certified Chinese Teachers
Materials: PDFs, Audio, Video, Quizzes

Course Structure:

1. Beginner Level (A1 - A2)
- Introduction to Pinyin & Chinese Characters (Hanzi)
- Basic Pronunciation & Tones in Mandarin
- Common Greetings & Self-Introduction
- Numbers, Dates, and Time Expressions
- Basic Grammar (Sentence Structure, Nouns, Verbs, and Measure Words)
- Simple Conversations for Daily Life (Ordering Food, Shopping, Directions)
- Basic Reading & Writing Practice
- Listening and Speaking Exercises

2. Intermediate Level (B1 - B2)
- Expanding Vocabulary & Sentence Complexity
- Grammar Focus (Past & Future Tenses, Sentence Patterns, Connectors)
- Conversational Practice (Work, Travel, Hobbies, and Social Situations)
- Writing Emails, Messages, and Short Essays in Chinese
- Reading & Comprehension (Short Stories, Articles, and News)
- Listening to Chinese Movies, Songs, and Podcasts
- Improving Pronunciation & Understanding Native Speakers
- Role-Playing and Interactive Speaking Activities

3. Advanced Level (C1 - C2)
- Fluency in Speaking & Expressing Ideas Naturally
- Advanced Grammar & Complex Sentence Structures
- Writing Formal Documents, Business Emails, and Academic Papers
- Understanding Complex Conversations & Chinese Idioms (成语)
- Learning Slang, Proverbs, and Cultural Expressions
- Public Speaking & Presentation Skills
- Discussion on Chinese Culture, History, and Social Issues
- Preparation for the HSK (Chinese Proficiency Test)

Additional Features:
✅ Weekly Quizzes & Assignments
✅ 1-on-1 Speaking Practice Sessions
✅ Certificate Upon Completion
✅ Interactive Group Activities

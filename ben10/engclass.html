Here’s a detailed course outline for an English Language Class on J Power Online Language Class:

English Language Course Details
Course Name: General English Course
Level: Beginner to Advanced
Duration: 3–6 months per level
Mode: Online (Live & Pre-recorded Classes)
Instructor: Certified English Teachers
Materials: PDFs, Audio, Video, Quizzes

Course Structure:

1. Beginner Level (A1 - A2)
- Introduction to English Alphabet & Pronunciation
- Basic Vocabulary & Sentence Structure
- Common Greetings & Everyday Conversations
- Numbers, Time, and Dates
- Basic Grammar (Nouns, Verbs, Adjectives, Articles, Prepositions)
- Simple Present & Past Tenses
- Listening and Speaking Practice
- Basic Writing Skills (Simple Sentences & Paragraphs)

2. Intermediate Level (B1 - B2)
- Expanding Vocabulary & Complex Sentences
- Grammar Focus (Present Perfect, Past Perfect, Future Tenses)
- Speaking Practice (Daily Life & Work Situations)
- Writing Emails, Essays, and Reports
- Reading & Comprehension (Articles, Stories, and News)
- Listening Skills (Podcasts, Interviews, Movies)
- Pronunciation Improvement & Accent Reduction
- Debates & Role-Playing Exercises

3. Advanced Level (C1 - C2)
- Fluency in Speaking & Expressing Ideas
- Advanced Grammar & Sentence Structures
- Formal Writing (Business, Academic, Professional)
- Understanding Native Speakers & Complex Conversations
- Idioms, Phrasal Verbs, and Slang Usage
- Public Speaking & Presentation Skills
- Critical Thinking & Discussion Topics
- Exam Preparation (TOEFL, IELTS, Cambridge Tests)

Additional Features:
✅ Weekly Quizzes & Assignments
✅ 1-on-1 Speaking Practice Sessions
✅ Certificate Upon Completion
✅ Interactive Group Activities

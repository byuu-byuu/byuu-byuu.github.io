Japanese Language Course

Here’s a detailed course outline for the Japanese Language Class on J Power Online Language Class:

Japanese Language Course Details
Course Name: General Japanese Course
Level: Beginner to Advanced
Duration: 3–6 months per level
Mode: Online (Live & Pre-recorded Classes)
Instructor: Certified Japanese Teachers
Materials: PDFs, Audio, Video, Quizzes

Course Structure:

1. Beginner Level (A1 - A2)
- Introduction to Hiragana & Katakana
- Basic Pronunciation & Writing Practice
- Common Greetings & Self-Introduction
- Numbers, Dates, and Time Expressions
- Basic Grammar (Particles, Verbs, Sentence Structure)
- Simple Conversations for Daily Life (Shopping, Ordering Food, Directions)
- Basic Kanji Introduction (First 100 Kanji)
- Listening and Speaking Practice

2. Intermediate Level (B1 - B2)
- Expanding Vocabulary & Sentence Complexity
- Grammar Focus (Verb Conjugations, Honorifics, Conditionals)
- Conversational Practice (Work, Travel, Social Situations)
- Writing Emails, Messages, and Short Essays in Japanese
- Reading & Comprehension (Short Stories, Articles, and News)
- Listening to Japanese Dramas, Songs, and Podcasts
- Kanji Expansion (300+ Kanji Characters)
- Role-Playing and Interactive Speaking Activities

3. Advanced Level (C1 - C2)
- Fluency in Speaking & Expressing Ideas Naturally
- Advanced Grammar & Complex Sentence Structures
- Writing Formal Documents, Business Emails, and Academic Papers
- Understanding Complex Conversations & Japanese Idioms (慣用句)
- Learning Slang, Proverbs, and Cultural Expressions
- Public Speaking & Presentation Skills
- Discussion on Japanese Culture, History, and Social Issues
- Preparation for JLPT (Japanese Language Proficiency Test - N5 to N1)

Additional Features:
✅ Weekly Quizzes & Assignments
✅ 1-on-1 Speaking Practice Sessions
✅ Certificate Upon Completion
✅ Interactive Group Activities

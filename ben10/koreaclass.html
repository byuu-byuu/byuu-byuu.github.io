Korean Language Course

Here’s a detailed course outline for the Korean Language Class on J Power Online Language Class:

Korean Language Course Details
Course Name: General Korean Course
Level: Beginner to Advanced
Duration: 3–6 months per level
Mode: Online (Live & Pre-recorded Classes)
Instructor: Certified Korean Teachers
Materials: PDFs, Audio, Video, Quizzes

Course Structure:

1. Beginner Level (A1 - A2)
- Introduction to Hangul (Korean Alphabet) & Pronunciation
- Basic Vocabulary & Simple Sentence Structures
- Common Greetings & Self-Introduction
- Numbers, Time, and Dates in Korean
- Basic Grammar (Particles, Nouns, Verbs, Adjectives)
- Sentence Patterns & Simple Conversations
- Basic Reading & Writing Practice
- Listening and Speaking Exercises

2. Intermediate Level (B1 - B2)
- Expanding Vocabulary & Complex Sentences
- Grammar Focus (Honorifics, Past & Future Tenses, Connectors)
- Conversational Practice (Daily Life, Shopping, Travel)
- Writing Emails, Messages, and Short Essays in Korean
- Reading & Comprehension (Articles, Short Stories, News)
- Listening to Korean Dramas, Songs, and Podcasts
- Pronunciation & Accent Improvement
- Role-Playing and Interactive Speaking Activities

3. Advanced Level (C1 - C2)
- Fluency in Speaking & Expressing Ideas Naturally
- Advanced Grammar & Sentence Structures
- Writing Formal Letters, Business Emails, and Academic Papers
- Understanding Native Speakers & Complex Conversations
- Learning Korean Idioms, Slang, and Proverbs
- Public Speaking & Presentation Skills
- Critical Thinking Discussions on Cultural & Social Topics
- Preparation for TOPIK (Test of Proficiency in Korean)

Additional Features:
✅ Weekly Quizzes & Assignments
✅ 1-on-1 Speaking Practice Sessions
✅ Certificate Upon Completion
✅ Interactive Group Activities

Thai Language Course

Here’s a detailed course outline for the Thai Language Class on J Power Online Language Class:

Thai Language Course Details
Course Name: General Thai Course
Level: Beginner to Advanced
Duration: 3–6 months per level
Mode: Online (Live & Pre-recorded Classes)
Instructor: Certified Thai Teachers
Materials: PDFs, Audio, Video, Quizzes

Course Structure:

1. Beginner Level (A1 - A2)
- Introduction to the Thai Alphabet (Consonants & Vowels)
- Basic Pronunciation & Tones in Thai
- Common Greetings & Self-Introduction
- Numbers, Days of the Week, and Time Expressions
- Basic Grammar (Sentence Structure, Nouns, Verbs, and Adjectives)
- Simple Conversations for Daily Life
- Basic Reading & Writing Practice
- Listening and Speaking Exercises

2. Intermediate Level (B1 - B2)
- Expanding Vocabulary & Sentence Complexity
- Grammar Focus (Tense, Sentence Particles, and Classifiers)
- Conversational Practice (Shopping, Travel, Food, and Work)
- Writing Simple Emails, Messages, and Short Essays in Thai
- Reading & Comprehension (Short Stories, Articles, and News)
- Listening to Thai TV Shows, Songs, and Podcasts
- Improving Pronunciation & Understanding Native Speakers
- Role-Playing and Interactive Speaking Activities

3. Advanced Level (C1 - C2)
- Fluency in Speaking & Expressing Ideas Naturally
- Advanced Grammar & Sentence Structures
- Writing Formal Documents, Business Emails, and Academic Papers
- Understanding Complex Conversations & Thai Slang
- Learning Thai Idioms, Proverbs, and Cultural Expressions
- Public Speaking & Presentation Skills
- Discussion on Thai Culture, History, and Social Issues
- Preparation for the Thai Proficiency Exam (CU-TFL, TU-GET, etc.)

Additional Features:
✅ Weekly Quizzes & Assignments
✅ 1-on-1 Speaking Practice Sessions
✅ Certificate Upon Completion
✅ Interactive Group Activities
